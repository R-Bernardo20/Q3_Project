The Weeknd

The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his unique blend of R&B, pop, and electronic music. The Weeknd has released several successful albums and singles, including "Starboy," "Blinding Lights," and "Die for You."
